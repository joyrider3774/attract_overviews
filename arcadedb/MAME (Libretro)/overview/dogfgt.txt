Arcade Video game published 36 years ago:

Acrobatic Dog-Fight (c) 1984 Data East USA.

Export version for North American release by Data East USA, under lisence from Technos Japan. For more information about the game itself, please see the original Japanese version; "Dog-Fight - Batten O'Hara no Sucharaka Kuuchuu-sen [Model TA-0011]".

- TRIVIA -

Antonio Colangelo of Senago, Italy holds the official record for this game with 1,445,050 points on July 17, 1985.
