Kong (c) 198? Taito do Brazil.

A bizarre Donkey Kong bootleg that runs on Namco's Galaxian hardware. There are some differences between Kong and Donkey Kong, as Kong shows the "Kong" logo upon bootup, and the jumping is a lot more jerky on Kong.
