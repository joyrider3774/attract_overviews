March 26, 1927, the starting gun is about to signal the drivers to begin a brand new race, an automobile race which was so enormously difficult to set up that its creators honestly believed that this race would be the first and last of its kind: the 1000 MIGLIA.

Be part of the famous 1000 miles rally that takes place every year in Italy using only 1930's race cars. This game has a poor sound quality and it is more like arcade game than racing game, but it's the first one that tried to implement a car behavior in a different racing conditions.
