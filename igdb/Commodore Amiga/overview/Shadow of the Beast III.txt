Maletoth remains a deadly threat; only when he's been utterly destroyed will you be wholly free from his malignant influence. in puny human form you now face the many testing challenges and powerful foes that stand between you and your final encounter with the Beast Lord. Featuring- side-view, multi-plexed hardware sprites; 800K of evocative music; hundreds of mind-blowing simultaneous on-screen colors; and full 8-way parallax scrolling. The last of the great trilogy, Shadow Of The Beast III is set for even greater accolades than its predecessors.
