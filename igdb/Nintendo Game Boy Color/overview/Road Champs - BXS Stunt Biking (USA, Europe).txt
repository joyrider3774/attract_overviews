Stunt biking makes an appearance on the Game Boy Color with ROAD CHAMPS BXS STUNT BIKING. When you start the game, you will have to progress through a 26-stage Training mode that teaches you how to perform all 50 moves in the game. After getting through training, you will be able take on a Single-Run, Career, or Tournament. The Single-Run mode allows you to work one course and master stringing tricks together, while Career mode has you compete in competitions to move from the amateur ranks to the world of the professionals. You can also compete in the Tournament mode to become the world champion. There are total of 64 unique levels spread throughout the game do you have what it takes to master ROAD CHAMPS BXS STUNT BIKING?
