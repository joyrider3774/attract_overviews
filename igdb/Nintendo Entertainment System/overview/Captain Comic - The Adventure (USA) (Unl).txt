Captain Comic: The Adventure is a classic 1988 MS-DOS platform game which is known for being one of the first side-scrolling games made for IBM PC. It was developed entirely by Michael Denio. The PC version of the game was distributed as shareware. Later a version for the NES was published by Color Dreams as an unlicensed title. An unofficial conversion for the SAM Coup√© also exists. It was also cloned as "The Adventures of Pioneer Ksenia" in 1990 by a team of Ukrainian programmers. Gameplay of Captain Comic: In the game, the player takes control of Captain Comic who is on a mission to the planet Tambi to recover three treasures stolen from the planet Omsoc.
