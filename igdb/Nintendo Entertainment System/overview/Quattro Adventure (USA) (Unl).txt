BOOMERANG KID: Amazing gameplay starring the Boomerang Kid and a host of weird creatures - snapping alligators, cute koalas, knights in armor, Toby the dog, plus castles, secret switches, crumbling platforms, hidden caves and world warps. All the action of the famous legend successfully captured in this amazing game. TREASURE ISLAND DIZZY: Dizzy's cartoon adventure on Treasure Island.
