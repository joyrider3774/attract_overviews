Secret of The Silver Blades is another brilliant Forgotten Realms RPG game from Strategic Simulations. Your primary goal is to locate the source of the mysterious hordes of monsters terrorizing the local people. Many seek to impede your efforts, such as deadly red dragons. There are also other forces who will aid you. The well of knowledge will give you many clues. Some of those clues will lead you to the dark and monster infested depths while others will reveal the true evil plans. Don't worry about your reward, along your path, you will recover a vast sum of gold and helpful items.
