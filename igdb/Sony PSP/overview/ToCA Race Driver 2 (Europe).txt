Toca Race Driver 2 is the sequel of the Codemasters' acclaimed Toca Race Driver. In this Game, you can race in about 15 different types of racing category, including official championships (like V8 Supercars and DTM), stock cars, supercars, rally, open wheels, supertruck category and much more .

In the single player career mode, you race for the success through various championships and tracks all over the world, challenging the world most talented drivers . You can play in online mode too, racing with up to 12 other opponents through either your PC internet connection or Xbox Live.
