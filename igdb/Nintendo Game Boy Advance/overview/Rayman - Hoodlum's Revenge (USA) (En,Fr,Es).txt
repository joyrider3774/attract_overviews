Rayman: Hoodlums' Revenge is an isometric platformer game based on Rayman 3: Hoodlum Havoc. 
 
A spin-off in the Rayman series, Hoodlum's Revenge takes place directly after the events from Rayman 3: Hoodlum Havoc and for the first in the series, aside from multi-player games, Rayman's friend Globox is featured as a playable character.
