Shogun Warriors, known in Japan as Fujiyama Buster (富士山バスター lit. "Mount Fuji Buster"?), is a 1992 fighting arcade game developed by Atop and Kaneko and published in Japan by Sammy Corporation and published in North America and Europe by Kaneko. It was the first modern-fighting game based on ancient Japanese mythology, and was created during the fighting game trend of the 1990s that began with Capcom's Street Fighter II.
