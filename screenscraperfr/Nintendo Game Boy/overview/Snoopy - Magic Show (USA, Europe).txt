As legendary magician Snoopy the Great, you must rescue your assistant Woodstock when his attempt to perform the dangerous Magic Ball Trick goes out of control. Magic Balls are on the attack and even Woodstock has been multiplied by the runaway spell. Maneuver around everchanging barriers and escape through enchanted exits. Summon all your powers to collect Woodstocks through more than 100 levels of escalating jeopardy in... Snoopy's Magic Show.
