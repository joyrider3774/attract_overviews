Fearless and Feisty

Join Mulan as she attempts to regain the honor of her family name. Disguised as a soldier, Mulan travels to The Imperial Army training camp to prove herself ready for battle. She must then set out to save the Empire by solving puzzles, battling enemies and ultimately defeating Shan-Yu in the final confrontation.

- Hours of action packed gameplay
- Many of your favorite characters from the movie
- Diverse gameplay including battles, swimming and "shieldboarding"
- A password system to save your progress
