BoxBoy! It is a puzzle platform video game in which players control a square-shaped character called Qbby. The objective of the game is to guide Qbby through a series of stages full of obstacles that are divided into sets called worlds. Each world focuses on a particular game theme; For example, one world introduces scenarios containing deadly lasers, and another introduces cranes to transport Qbby. The central mechanics of the game is Qbby's ability to create boxes and use them to remove obstacles. The boxes are created one at a time as a connected chain and the maximum number of boxes in a chain varies between each stage.
