As though the bugs, bees, bats, and bears weren't enough, now the Alien Slime Beings have made off with your girlfriend. Now it's up to you, nobody else can save her. Gather cans and bottles, they earn you needed weapons and protections and they might just come in handy along the way. Collect power crystals. You'll need them! Find the secret code to enter the alien camp. Fight your way through flames, froads, and flying campers to rescue your girlfriend. You haven't much time, but you can do it!
