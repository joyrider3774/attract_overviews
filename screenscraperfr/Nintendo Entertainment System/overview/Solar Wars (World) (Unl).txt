Solar Wars is a tank combat game like Scorched Earth on the PC, or Scorched Tanks on the Amiga. The game is two-player and pits you against a foe throughout our solar system. Here are some of the features in Solar Wars:

Fight against a friend using two controllers, or the same controller.
Fight on ten different worlds, each with its own gravity, atmosphere, and climate.
Multiple types and looks of terrain.
64 different screens worth of randomly-selected terrain.
4 different endings.
Pumpin' soundtrack.
Vibrant colours.
