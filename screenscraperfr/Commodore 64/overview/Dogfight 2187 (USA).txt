In the 22nd Century, a grave crisis face the universe. A hole in the space time continuum has allowed hordes of marauding alien invaders access to our universe, and now, only one man stands between them and us: Rhett Dexter! In Dogfight 2187, you are Dexter and must search the heavens for the pieces of the spacial generator. These pieces were left by the Old Ones, wich when assembled, will seal the breach. But between you and the generator stand untold numbers of deadly alien craft intent on one purpose only... preventing you from sealing the hole in space... thus insuring their inevitable victory and total domination of the Free Universe.
