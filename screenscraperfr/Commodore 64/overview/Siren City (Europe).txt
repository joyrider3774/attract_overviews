Siren City is one of the roughest towns in the whole of the U.S.A. Starting as a rookie driving through a maze of Skyscrapers and seemingly peaceful streets your job is to keep law & order but you are dealing with some of the toughest criminals not behind bars. These include Public Enemy No1 : Slit-Throat Steve, Public Enemy No 7 : Dune Buggy Jon and Dr J. Davies Ph.D, V.E.A.S.
