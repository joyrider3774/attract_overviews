You are a dim ignorant Apprentice Enchanter, but your task is enormous.  You must save the world from a Warlock who is ruling the land with his evil powers.  You are not the first in this quest but hopefully you will be the first to succeed.  
You know  the basics of magic and will have the chance to learn many spells. Keep your wits and use everything you can to your benefit.

Difficulty Level: Standard
