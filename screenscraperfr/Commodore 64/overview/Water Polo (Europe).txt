How's your swimming ? As good as your computer skills we hope 'coz if they are not up to scratch you'll soon find yourself padding round the deep end with nowhere else to go.
Behind the frantic action and hilarious spectacle of men imitating ducks lies the ball handling skills of a footballer, the physical endurance of an athlete and the tactical brain of a cricketer - all set in a huge tub of water.
Waterpolo is an amazing combination of strategy, skill and team play that's guaranteed to fascinate the sports player and computer enthusiast alike.
