You can experience the future of racing today with JEFF GORDON XS RACING. Choose from five different cars and race on seven different tracks. You can use the Time Trial mode to get the fastest lap time possible on an empty track. In the Championship mode, you will have to race through two seasons. The first season has you racing against five other cars, and you must finish in first place overall to gain access to the second season. In the second season you will face seven other competitors, including Jeff Gordon. Win the second season, and you can challenge Gordon to a one-on-one race on any track you choose. Should your friends want in on the action, they can join you via a link cable. Get behind the wheel of a futuristic racecar and take first place in JEFF GORDON XS RACING.
