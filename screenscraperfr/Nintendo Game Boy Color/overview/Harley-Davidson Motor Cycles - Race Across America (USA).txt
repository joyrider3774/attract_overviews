Live the life of an easy rider in HARLEY DAVIDSON: Race Across America. There are four bikes to choose from, and they all of them have the classic Harley Davidson shape and sound. You can go for a high-speed, low-acceleration model, or if you prefer to burn rubber off of the line, you will want to choose a bike with monster acceleration. When you are ready to ride you will have the opportunity to race on four different tracks including the Arizona Desert, Deep South, Florida Everglades, and California Coast.
