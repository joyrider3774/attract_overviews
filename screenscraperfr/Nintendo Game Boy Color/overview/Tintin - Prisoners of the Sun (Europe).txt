A thousand curses ! A bizarre illness has stricken several explorers upon their return from Peru. Tintin is leading the investigation, when Professor Calculus mysteriously disappears...
It's the beginning of a thrilling adventure that will lead Tintin to the deepest heart of the Andes Cordillera. Your adventures in the land of the Incas promise to be action-packed and will test your wits! Will you discover the secret of the Temple of the Sun?
