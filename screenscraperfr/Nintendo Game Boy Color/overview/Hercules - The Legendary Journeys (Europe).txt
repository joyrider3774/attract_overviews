Are you a true God?

You are Hercules, son of Zeus, and you must prove you are a true God to mortal eyes. Get ready for an extraordinary journey! This thrilling game, based on world-known TV series by Universal, mixes fast-paced action and adventure.

-Wide range of characters and adventures riddled with several missions and pitfalls.
-Link mode: play with your friends and exchange characters if you have Titus game Xena on Game Boy Color; Play as Xena in Hercules game and as Hercules in Xena game. You will access to hidden worlds.
