Do you think you can take the WCW championship? Now you can try in WCW MAYHEM. There are 12 WCW superstars you can compete with or against including Sting, Goldberg, Hollywood Hogan, Kevin "Big Sexy" Nash, Randy "Macho Man" Savage, Diamond Dallas Page, Bret Hart, Bam Bam Bigelow, Ric Flair, Buff Bagwell, Booker T., and Lex Luger. All of the wrestlers have special characteristics and unique finishing moves. Take on any of the wrestlers in the Player vs. Computer mode, or you can try to win the championship in the Quest for the Best mode. You can also challenge your friends to a match in the Player vs. Player mode. In all of the modes, you can take the action outside of the ring, giving you the full WCW treatment. Are you tough enough to stay alive and win the WCW championship with WCW MAYHEM?
