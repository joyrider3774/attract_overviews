Fighters' Impact is 3D fighting game. The player can choose 1 from 8 playable characters (the game has a couple of bosses too) and fights against the others. Game has special combo system (players can come up with their own combos), and characters have a special sub-styles of fighting. The game uses 3 buttons: for punch, kick and evade. Rules are simple - the player must beat the opponent in 2 rounds.
