NBA Jam Extreme was produced by Acclaim in 1996. Acclaim released 5 different machines in our database under this trade name, starting in 1996. Other machines made by Acclaim during the time period NBA Jam Extreme was produced include Batman Forever, Judge Dredd, Magic The Gathering: Armageddon, and ReVolt. Play two-on-two basketball using NBA teams that consist of six players each. The game play is three-dimensional instead of using the common 2D format. Game commentary is provided by Marv Albert.
