NHK Taiga Drama: Taiheiki is a strategy game exclusive to the PC Engine that was published by the video game division of NHK, a national broadcasting corporation in Japan. They had previously adapted their TV show Niko Niko Pun for the system the previous year.nnnnIt is based on one of NHK's annual hour-long historical fiction TV shows, collectively known as Taiga Drama, specifically the 1991 episode based on Taiheiki, a novel from the 14th century that is set between the years of 1319 to 1367 and concerns a war between the Southern Court of Emperor Go-Daigo in Yoshino and the Northern Court of Ashikaga Takauji in Kyoto
