Shogi Shodan Icchokusen ("Shogi First-Dan Straight Line") is a Shogi game for the PC Engine released by Home Data (which was renamed Magical Company in 1993) in 1990.The game features two modes:The standard mode involves playing various NPCs at shogi at various difficulty levels.The story mode involves playing through a campaign as the legendary Japanese folk hero Momotaro, as he competes with oni and other foes at shogi. In this mode the player's cursor switches to Momotaro's dog companion.
