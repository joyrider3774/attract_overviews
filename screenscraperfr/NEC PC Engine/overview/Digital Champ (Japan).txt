Set in the near future, DIGITAL CHAMP is a first-person-perspective boxing title that plunges players into the raw intensity of the ring! Determined to thwart Mother Computers plot to conquer mankind, you infiltrate the enemys domain, using your boxing skills to vanquish your foes. Each round is three minutes, with a total of twelve rounds to a match. Advance to the next contest through either a KO or by flattening an opponent three times in a round for a TKO. Utilize an arsenal of left and right jabs, hooks, power-charged crosses, and three different kinds of Devastator punches. Deftly block and slip your opponents attacks, and look for your chance to lay them out. Master these techniques and take down the greatest opponent of them all, DIGITAL CHAMP!
