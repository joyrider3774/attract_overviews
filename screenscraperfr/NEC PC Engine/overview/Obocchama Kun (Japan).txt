From the manga of the same name, you will play Chama Obo, a young boy from a rich Japanese family. One day, a huge mountain in the shape of a turtle appears. It is said that this mountain was the home of the Obo ancestors and that a fabulous treasure would be found there. According to legend, the eruption of this mountain announces however a bad omen and would be initiated by a profanation. The young Chama does not support this idea and promises his father, ready to call the army, he will kick the buttocks intruders. With his friends, he goes to the mountains, in a hurry to fight. Unfortunately, soon after the beginning of their journey, his companions are removed by dark forces ... and that was not planned in the program!
