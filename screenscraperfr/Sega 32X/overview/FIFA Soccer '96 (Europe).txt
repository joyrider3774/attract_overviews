FIFA Soccer 96 featured revolutionary commentary for its time -- the renowned British commentator John Motson covers all the the action on the pitch in real-time.

FIFA Soccer 96 boasts 11 leagues from around the world, ranging from unknown players in the Malaysian league to the celebrated stars of the English and Italian leagues. 59 International teams are available for friendlies tournaments (including the World Cup). You can also create your own dream team through the custom team creator.
