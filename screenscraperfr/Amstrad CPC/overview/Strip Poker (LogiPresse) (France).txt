You are a certain man, playing against a girl called Marilyn over 20 rounds of a poker game. In each round you have to change up to 4 cards if you've already got the "Ace" to acquire the poker's winning combination. Marylin have to do the same after you. After comparing both hands, the winner is defined. Depending on this, Marilyn strips off or get dressed. You will need six winning hands, one after another, to achieve your goal.

The game is played in French.
