Press left arrow key to play game A (easy). Press right arrow key to play game B (hard). Tom is flying balloon bombs! Break them quickly by the saber. It's Jerry's mistake when you failed to break balloons or the saber was taken by the magnet (in game B only). The game ends when you make 3 mistakes. 1 point per broken balloon 1 point per broken magnet (game B only) Maximum display score is 999 points.
