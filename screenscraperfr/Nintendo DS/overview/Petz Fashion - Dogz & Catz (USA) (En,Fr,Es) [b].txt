Design cute and stylish outfits for your puppy or kitten, dress them up and enter them in fashion shows with Petz Fashion: Dogz & Catz. Choose your kitten or puppy from a large variety of breeds, care for them and develop a strong relationship with them. Use the stylus to design unique patterns and pick from different colors to create cute outfits for your pet kitten or puppy. You can also choose from a variety of trendy outfits and accessories suited to your pet's liking. Dress up your pet and show off your creation in fashion contests, and work your way up to become a posh pet clothing designer. Unlock new outfits, accessories and items for your house with money earned from your fashion success.
