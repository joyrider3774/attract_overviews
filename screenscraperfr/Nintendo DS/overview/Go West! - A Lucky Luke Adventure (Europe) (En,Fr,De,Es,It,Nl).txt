Join Lucky Luke, The Pioneers and The Dalton Brothers to relive the "Go West" adventure. You will have to travel the USA from East to West in less than 80 days by playing great party games. Will you succeed?

Be the bad guy as Joe Dalton and rob a bank!
Communicate with fearsome Indians in order to cross the Deadly Desert of Death!
Be the good guy as Lucky Luke and chase The Daltons through the streets of New York!
