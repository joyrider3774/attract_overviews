NARUTO superstars collide in the series' most overthetop handheld fighting game yet! Fight as Naruto, Sasuke, and your other favorite characters using an exciting, easytolearn battle system full of acrobatics and groundshaking combos. In addition to a personal story mode for each of the game's 16 fighters, nearly two dozen overarching story fights, an assortment of secret missions, and a wealth of unlockable content offer tremendous variety, replay value, and fun!
