In Dream Salon you play as Jenny, a recent graduate of the New York Beauty Academy, and help build her career as she styles model after model, creating the ultimate fashionistas! Every aspect of hair, makeup, and fashion is unique and customizable, so create the total superstar package! Whip up a cutting edge hair do, complete with color and highlights. Use the latest cosmetics and trendiest colors so your models put their best face forward. Design fashion-forwards outfits for runway shows that will put your models on the cover of magazines worldwide.
