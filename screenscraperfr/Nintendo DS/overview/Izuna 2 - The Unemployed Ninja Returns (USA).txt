The hilarious adventure of everyone's favorite jobless ninja sets the stage for old school dungeon RPG play! When Shina suddenly runs off in search of her long-lost sister, Izuna must track her down AND try to avoid a slew of angry gods spoiling for a fight. Over a dozen playable characters: Choose from the wackiest cast ever, including enemy bosses from the first game. TAG Team System: Take any two party members into a dungeon and unleash fierce TAG team attacks.
