May's Mystery: Forbidden Memories is an exciting puzzle adventure that follows May, the intelligent and charismatic girl in her quest to find her missing brother Tery. Advance through the adventure, meet curious characters, visit peculiar places and unravel the mysteries by solving puzzles. With over 270 puzzles to solve, including logic puzzles, rhythmic games and hand drawn hidden objects scenes, you will be captivated and immersed in the wonderful world of May's Mystery.
