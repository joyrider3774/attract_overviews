Petz, the #1 handheld pet-simulation brand in 2010, introduces an all-new game where you can care for and love the most adorable puppy and kitten duo as you help them grow and become best friends. Watch out, though, because some breeds will need more time and nurturing before they become good friends! With a wide variety of fun activities and different pet personalities, these Petz will never cease to amaze you. Feed them, help them grow, and watch the adorableness unravel as the puppies and kittens grow and play together.
