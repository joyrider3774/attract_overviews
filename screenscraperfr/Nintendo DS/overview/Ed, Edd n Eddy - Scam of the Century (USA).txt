In Ed, Edd n Eddy: Scam of the Century, the always-scheming Eds have found their Book O' Scams stolen and in the hands of none other than their enemy Captain Melonhead and the other kids of the cul-de-sac. Now that the Eds' schemes are exposed, the rival kids are dEDD-set on turning the scamming plans against the Eds. All the popular characters, items and locations from the much-loved animated show on Cartoon Network are part of the game, as well as original content for all-new experiences with Ed, Edd and Eddy.
