Master the art of drilling and take advantage of the dual screens and wireless connectivity of the Nintendo DS. Your fellow drillers are trapped underground, and it's up to you to dig through the blocks to rescue them. Complete various gameplay modes to unlock new playable characters. With quick play features, you can compete head-to-head against other players using the DS's wireless capabilities. Whether it's racing to avoid falling blocks or scrambling for an air capsule, Mr. Driller Drill Spirits offers a variety of puzzle-solving challenges.
