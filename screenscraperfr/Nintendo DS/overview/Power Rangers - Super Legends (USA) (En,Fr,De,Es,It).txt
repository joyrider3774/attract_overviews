Protect the world from evil villains, anywhere you go! The long-running, hit television show, "Power Rangers" now comes to the Nintendo DS in the all new game, Power Rangers: Super Legends. Playing as one of sixteen Rangers from the Mighty Morphin era to the current Operation Overdrive team, fans will travel across time and have the opportunity to unlock powerful special moves and devastating super attacks while battling hordes of henchmen and powerful bosses.
