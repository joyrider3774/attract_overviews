Take parties and family game night to a whole new level with Zoo Games Minute to Win It, based on NBCs hit game show sensation. With the encouragement of All-American chef and TV personality Guy Fieri, who provides voiceovers for the game, players compete in ten, 60-second challenges to win the virtual grand prize of $1,000,000. Use everyday household objects to conquer these simple yet nerve-wracking challenges to win each round. Featuring both Solo and Multiplayer modes, and 36 fun and exciting challenges, Minute to Win It is the ultimate party game for all ages!
