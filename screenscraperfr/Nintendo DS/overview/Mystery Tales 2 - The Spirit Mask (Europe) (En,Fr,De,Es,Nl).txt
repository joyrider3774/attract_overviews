The player is the witch Melanie Cygnes from the first game. This time she finds a strange Japanese mask in Venice which contains a mysterious spirit.

Melanie investigates the mask and finds out all kind of contradicting information about it. What's in the mask? A demon? An angel? Something else? The only way to solve the mystery is to start following the history of the mask thru time . She visits Tibet, China, Malaysia and Japan, has plenty of adventures and meet a lot of people. Can you help Melanie solving the mystery behind the mask?
