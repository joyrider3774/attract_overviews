Jump into the hippity hoppity world of Universal Pictures' live action/CG-animated comedy, HOP! Play as Fred as you help E.B. save Easter from a fluffy coup d'etat led by Carlos and his fellow chick workers! Hop your way through the Easter Bunny's top secret candy factory on Easter Island, solve puzzles and sugarshock your enemies as you turn them into delicious chocolate treats. Filled with a collection of mouth-watering mini-games, this action adventure puzzle game is big enough to stuff in any Easter basket.
