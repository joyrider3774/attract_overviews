The Amazing Spider-Man is an open world action-adventure video game,based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man, and the 2012 film of the same name. It was developed by Beenox and published by Activision.
Set several months after the events of the film, the game sees Spider-Man temporarily releasing Curt Connors from prison to help find a cure for the people infected by the unstable and dangerous cross-species created by Oscorp using Connors' research, while also facing Alistair Smythe, the new CEO of Oscorp, who desires to save the city himself and will stop from nothing to achieve his goal, but ends up only causing more problems for the city.
