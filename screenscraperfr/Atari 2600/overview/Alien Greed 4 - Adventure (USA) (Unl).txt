After a two-year rest on your home planet, it is time to find some more Atari 2600 cartridges. It is time to enter a new world !
What your elders failed to tell you is that this new world is filled with vicious, blood thirsty ducks! OK, they are really dragons, but they sure do look like ducks! These dragons are protecting and defending the Atari 2600 cartridges.
