This is a mahjongg tile-matching collection pack of 4 games.  A player can also choose which type of mahjongg game they wish to play and which tile-set to use. The tile sets include: a set made from art masterpieces, American folk songs, famous lovers, great scientists/inventions, and old Sci-fi films. When a tile is matched with its partner-tile, an animated video and audio clip is played. Players can also play in Solitaire mode, 2-player mode or tournaments.
