Super Ice Hockey (JP: Super Hockey '94) is a Super Nintendo hockey game from Japanese developers Opera House. The game uses a Mode 7 slanted top-down perspective of the rink, and features national teams from across the world, including: Russia, Czech Republic, USA, Canada, Sweden, Norway, Finland, Germany, France, Switzerland, United Kingdom, Italy, Austria, China, Japan and Australia.

Super Ice Hockey was released in Japan by Party Room 21 (a.k.a. Yonezawa PR21) in March of 1994. It was published in Europe in December of the same year by SunSoft.
