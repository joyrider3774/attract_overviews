Alien 3 on Super NES immerses you immediately after the end of the movie "Aliens". Ellen Ripley, the main character in the saga, is dormant cryogenically aboard the Sulaco Ship. An alien has infiltrated on board, decimating the crew and causing the ship to crash on the planet Fiorina 161. Help Ripley to survive and rescue the prisoners from the clutches of the aliens until help arrives.
