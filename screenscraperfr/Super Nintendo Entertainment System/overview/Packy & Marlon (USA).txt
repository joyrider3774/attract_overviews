Oh no! Packy and Marlon, two pachyderm pals with diabetes, are in heavy trouble. Summer camp has been raided by rats! The pesky critters have stolen food and critical diabetes supplies. Can Packy and Marlon find the food and supplies? To do it, they have to blast vicious varmints with peanuts and water. They have to remember to eat healthy stuff, check blood glucose levels, and take insulin!
