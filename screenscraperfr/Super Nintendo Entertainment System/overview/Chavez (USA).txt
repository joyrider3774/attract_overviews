Julio César Chávez is undefeated. You want a piece of him? You'll get your shot, as this game allows you to create your fighter and rise through the ranks against fighters of all styles. The 65 fighters include a list of 25 ranked opponents each with a unique approach to boxing. In Career mode, your fighter will actually age throughout the course of the game, and complete stats on you efforts are recorded. You'll go through rigorous training between bouts, each session designed to help you against Bowe, himself. You'll get plenty advice from Chávez, but that doesn't mean he'll go easy on you when your time comes to square off against him.
