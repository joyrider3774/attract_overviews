Minna no Taabou no Nakayoshi Daisakusen is a puzzle game released in Japan on the 8-bit Nintendo NES console. It's actually an improved memory game. You play as a man in a tray where his present a number of cards. Your goal is simple: find the pairs. There are enemies around you, and you'll have to be careful not to touch them. As you progress through the levels, the number of cards and enemies increases, and bricks appear to create a labyrinth and force you to take paths a little more delicate.
