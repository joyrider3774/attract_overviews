Challenge Tradition! Beat a friend electronically for practice. Then go for it against the Ultimate Checker Player - a Wiley computer who asks and gives no quarter.

- Two ways to play, vs. a friend or the ultimate competitor, the canny computer.
- HI and LO skill levels when playing the computer. Added difficulty... added excitement.
- "Bail Out" button - COMPUTER SUGGESTS MOVE, when you're stumped against the computer.
- Simulated sound effects - BELLS, BUZZERS, A BRONX CHEER and a VICTORY TUNE.
- No time limit on moves!
- CLEAR button that lets you change your move!
