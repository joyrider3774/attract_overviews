Zen-Nippon Pro Wrestling 2: 3-4 Budoukan is a wrestling game for the Super Famicom and the fourth and final game from Natsume to be based on the Japanese wrestling organization that is best known outside of Japan as All-Japan Pro Wrestling (or AJPW).nnnnIt features a larger roster and returning modes from previous games, and introduces Budokan mode, essentially a booking mode where the player tries to receive a good rating for a wrestling card.
