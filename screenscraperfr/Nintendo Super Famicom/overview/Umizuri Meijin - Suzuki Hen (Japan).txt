Umi Tsuri Meijin: Suzuki Hen ("Sea Fishing Expert: Suzuki Chapter". Suzuki is the Japanese name for Japanese seabass (Lateolabrax japonicus)) is a fishing game for the Super Famicom. It was published in 1994 by EA Sports Victor, EA's Japanese branch, and developed by A-Wave.

The game features sea fishing, with the player selecting venues - and then various spots within that venue - from which to catch various species of sea fish; the most prominent of which is the eponymous Suzuki.
