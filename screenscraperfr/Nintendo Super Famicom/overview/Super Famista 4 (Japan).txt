Super Famista 4 is a baseball game for the Super Famicom. It is the fourth game for the system, and the thirteenth game in the series overall. As with its forebears, the game features the real teams of the Nippon Professional Baseball league and a super-deformed style for its athletes. The game also features in-game advertising for the first time, for Kirin beverages.

The game features one-game play, three-game play, league, all-star and tryout modes.
