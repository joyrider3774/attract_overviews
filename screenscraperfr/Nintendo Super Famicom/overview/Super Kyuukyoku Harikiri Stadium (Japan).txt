Super Kyuukyoku Harikiri Stadium ("Super Ultimate Excitement Stadium") is a Baseball game published by Taito for the Super Famicom platform. Super Kyuukyoku Harikiri Stadium is the first game in the Kyuukyoku Harikiri Stadium franchise that was released on the Super Famicom. Like its predecessors, it presents a view from behind the batter and switches to an overhead view of the field once the ball is hit.nnnnIt was followed with a sequel: Super Kyuukyoku Harikiri Stadium 2.
