Tsuppari Oozumou: Risshin Shussehen ("Shoving Sumo: Success in Career") is a sumo wrestling game and the sequel to Tecmo's Tsuppari Oozumou for the Famicom. The player takes a sumo wrestler, names them and takes them through competitions to build their reputation and standing in the sumo world.

A secret code inputted on the main menu allows the player to play as a blonde woman wearing a spare tire around her midriff.
