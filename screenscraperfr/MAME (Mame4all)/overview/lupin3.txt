The player has to guide Lupin (who does not resemble the thief) in a maze. The goal is to steal the money bags from the top of the screen towards the bottom while avoiding guards and guard dogs. There is a move where the player can move out of the way however this deduces your score.nnnnAfter the end of every stage, a cutscene where Lupin in a classic car travels towards the park and meets Fujiko. After completing three stages, she falls in love with Lupin. In the International version, Fujiko declares her love to Lupin after completing the forth stage.
