Medarot: Perfect Edition is a RPG developed by Natsume and published by Imagineer in 1999 for the WonderSwan, and is a "director's cut" edition of the original game released on the Nintendo Game Boy in 1997.
It follows Hikaru Agata on his quest to compete in Robottle tournaments and defeat the evil Roborobo Gang. It was released in two versions: Kabuto (Rhinoceros beetle), where the player's starting Medarot is Metal Beetle, and Kuwagata (Stag beetle), where the player starts with Head Scissors.
