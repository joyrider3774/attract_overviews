The evil Baron Lazarus hid your family treasures in th tombs beneath the derelict Castle, and for 200 years his Wolf-Spirit has kept ghostly guard. You must brave the 10 challenging Tomb Levels and regain the family fortune ... stay alive! (... and it's about time you learned somme manners, too!)
