Miki is a timed platform game. The cruel sorcerer attacked a quiet kingdom. The player must defeat the enemy through the passage of eleven levels full of hostile animals and deadly traps. Reaching the next level requires to find a key and the gate before the time runs out.The enemies may be killed by throwing the rocks or jumping on their heads. Labyrinths are full of useful items, such as shoes (temporary resistance to spikes), golden heads (temporary resistance to enemies), extra time, extra life or extra rocks. Some parts of the level may be reached by using a proper floppy disk on the teleporting gates.
