Your training was tough. Now is the time for your FINAL MISSION in Labyrinth of terror  the last stage of insanity.

Many before you have tried, all have failed. The last one was found impaled in a pitfall with his 2x10 caliber game gun between the bones. The Labyrinth want you dead! You must destroy the spirit of the Labyrinth. The red and yellow balls of madness and insanity, touch them and you are closer to freedom. Dangerous mines, traps and energy fields will push you to the limit. Will your 
FINAL MISSION become your final disaster? Was your training in vain?
