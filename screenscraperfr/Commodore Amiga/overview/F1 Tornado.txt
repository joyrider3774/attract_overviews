The F1 Tornado flies from the allies aircraft carrier based on the edge of the Gulf battle zone. Your mission is to seek out and destroy all enemy installations, frigates, aircraft, tanks and gun emplacements.
 
As well as oil-cooled machines fire, the F1 Tornado is also well armed with Target Allocating Missiles capable of destroying sea level enemy installations. All weapon systems are controlled via the Head Up Computer Display. Prepare for battle, you have only three chances to complete your mission... Best of luck...
