Cyber Kick is a science fiction platform game. The action takes place in the space base of renegades on Mars. John Malkovitch goal is to collect all the radioactive containers and put them in the recycling stations. The player will encounter countless number of traps, such as electric barriers, sharp blades or vices and hostile opponents, such as alien creatures, drones, mechs, tanks and others. The only weapon of the player is a powerful cyber kick. In base teleporters can be activated only after finding the right access card.
