They all ignored the endless warnings. Now Earth is dying and it is your task to find a new and flourishing planet.

Exodus provides us with an excellent blend of role playing strategy and 3D space combat. It's also fairly easy to get into and you will soon be building impulse systems and the like with the best of them. Although the combat system may be fairly simple, don't be misled as it serves its purpose well, and in combination with the other many fine aspects, they add up to one first class game.
