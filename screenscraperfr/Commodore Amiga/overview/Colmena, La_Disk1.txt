"La Colmena" was one of the last releases of Opera Soft and one of the few commercial erotic games edited in Spain.

This videogame is inspired by an erotic table game (The Pyramid of Love), the purpose of which is to join together several adult people. During the course of the game, the participants will have to surpass certain tests and challenges.  In the later portions of game, the tests will be more and more stimulating, and the participants will be taking off their clothes.

La Colmena has many different subgames, like the roulette, blackjack, poker, etc. and during the game many general thematic questions need to be answered. The game has tons of humor too.
