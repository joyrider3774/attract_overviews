Answer Back: Senior Quiz is a Quiz game, developed and published by Kosmos, which was released in 1990.



Quizzes are popular with everyone. Quizzes combined with Space-Age sound and colorful graphics are irresistible. Quizzes containing a vast selection of well-researched questions have immense educational value. The ANSWER BACK quiz provides a unique adventure in education by allowing the successful challenger to participate in a simple and compelling game.
