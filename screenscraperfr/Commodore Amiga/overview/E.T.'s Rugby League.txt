E.T's Rugby League is fondly remembered by gamers of the era and for many would still represent the pinnacle of rugby league video games. While at its heart E.T's Rugby League is just World Class Rugby with the six tackle rule, adding a play the ball and removing two players per side takes the experience from good to great. You get more room to throw the ball around, offloading becomes even more deadly, half breaks are more frequent and your joystick lives a longer, happier life for not being violently waggled every ten seconds.
