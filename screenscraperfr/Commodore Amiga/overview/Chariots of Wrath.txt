Chariots of Wrath is Action game, developed by Toxic Waste and published by Impressions, which was released in Europe in 1989.

While fighting for your King in far off lands, a rival Baron has kidnapped Princess Arthena, your future Queen. On hearing this, enraged, you wearily climb into your trusty chariot to save her and rid your Kingdom of this scourge for evermore. Chariots of Wrath takes arcade games into the next generation - combining the very best in arcade action with the depth and challenge usually only offered by adventures or strategy games. It gives you more action, more destructive weaponry, more variety, more traps, more aliens, more surprises! In short, Chariots of Wrath means quite simply more excitement!
