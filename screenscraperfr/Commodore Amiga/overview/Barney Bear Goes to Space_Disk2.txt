Barney Bear Goes to Space is a colorful, musical game for children ages 3-8.
The simple interface uses animation, games, and stories to present facts about space that even young children can enjoy without adult supervision.
Barney Bear and his class are on a trip to Cape Canaveral, Florida to watch the shuttle launch. While touring Mission Control, Barney Bear falls asleep on a conveyer and finds himself inside the shuttle! This short interactive story takes Barney Bear to the control panel of the shuttle, where there are stories, facts, and games to play.
