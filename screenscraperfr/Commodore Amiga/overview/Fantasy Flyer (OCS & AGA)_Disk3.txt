Fantasy Flyer is 2D side scrolling action game. The player controls a flying, dragon-like creature with big eyes, flaming lizard female and wall crawling gnome. The fly and walk, exploring level, kill strange enemies, looks for special objects (like chariot's parts) to be able to move on, and avoids various traps. The creatures have a limited amount of shots. Game uses big 2D sprites (for 16-bit platform).
