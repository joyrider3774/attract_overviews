Become the lovable nerd Larry for one fabulous night. You'll dance, you'll drink, you'll gamble. And, if you play your cards right, you might even meet the girl (or girls) of your dreams! Leisure Suit Larry is a humorous, harmless endeavor for adults. The object of the game is to help Larry overcome his jerkisms and lose his "you-know-what." It's a silly, risque romp through the singles scene and a challenging adventure game that will test your street smarts and suave sophistication. So, slip into your leisure suit and venture out into "the land of the lounge lizards." We guarantee it'll be a night you will never forget!
