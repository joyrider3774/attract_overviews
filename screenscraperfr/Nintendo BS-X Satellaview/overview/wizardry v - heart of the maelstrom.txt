Despite the greatest magic of the ancient High Sages, great floods, earthquakes and famine again pervade the land of Llylgamyn. The great Orb of Llylgamyn, an artifact of remarkable power that has protected the city for ages, is now powerless to halt the scourge.

But now, the Sages have discovered that the hidden reason behind the disasters is more frightening than anyone could have imagined. To save the very world as we know it, you and your party must plunge into the..................Heart of The Maelstrom.
