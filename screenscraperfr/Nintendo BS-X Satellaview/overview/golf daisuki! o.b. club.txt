Golf Daisuki! O.B. Club is a view-limited Downloadable golfing game for the Satellaview initially running between August 10, 1997 and August 23, 1997. The game was rebroadcast for at least 4 runs ending in March 30, 1998.nnElectronic Gaming Monthly magazine produced a preview of this game. Their preview had an English-language logo even though the game was never released in the USA in any form.
