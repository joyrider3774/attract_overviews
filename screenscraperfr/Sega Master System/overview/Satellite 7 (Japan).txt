Satellite 7 is a shoot-'em-up game developed and published by Sega. It was released for the Sega Master System exclusively in Japan on Sega Card in 1985.nnnnThe game is a fairly standard shooter and is very similar to Namco's Xevious. 1 launches bombs at ground targets, and 2 shoots forward at airborne targets.nnnnThough the game did not officially leave Japan in card form, the game has appeared in several compilations in Brazil, including the Tectoy DVD Karaoke Game DVT-G100.
