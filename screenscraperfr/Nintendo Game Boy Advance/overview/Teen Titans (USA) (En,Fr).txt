Based on the hit show and DC Comics series, Teen Titans gives gamers the chance to be any of the Teen Titans heroes as they switch in realtime between heroes Robin, Starfire, Beast Boy, Cyborg and Raven to make use of the Teen Titans? unique moves and abilities. The notorious villains Slade, Overload, Jinx, Gizmo, and Mammoth will all be out, prowling this crime-ridden city and causing mayhem for the teen superheroes.
