Now you can watch the wild underwater antics of SpongeBob SquarePants on your Game Boy Advance. Volume 3 of the SpongeBob SquarePants video series includes four episodes from the cartoon: "Walking Small," "Mermaidman & Barnacleboy II," "Hooky," and "Texas." In this collection, you'll see SpongeBob do everything from summoning superheroes to riding fishing hooks.
