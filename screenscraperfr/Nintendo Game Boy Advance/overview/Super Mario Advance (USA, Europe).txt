Super Mario Advance is the adaptation on GBA of Super Mario Bros 2 released on NES. This episode marks a turning point in the series as it introduces the possibility of choosing his character. Embody the famous tandem of mustachio plumbers, Princess Peach or her companion Toad in a magical adventure. You must save the inhabitants of the dream world from the clutches of the ignoble Wart, the batrachian tyrant. This version offers as a bonus a revamped version of the game Mario Bros.
