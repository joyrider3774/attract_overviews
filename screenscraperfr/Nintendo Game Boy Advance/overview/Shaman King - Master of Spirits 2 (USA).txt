Step into the world of Yoh Asakura as he trains to become the next Shaman King in this exciting action RPG. Challenged by the powerful will of Zeke, Yoh must summon all his abilities if he is to succeed and prevail. Collect the spirits of fallen enemies, use their special powers to navigate terrifying traps and find special items, and discover a world of surprises! As players progress they will build their skills, unlock special moves, combo systems, and abilities in the battle between Zeke and Yoh, or good and evil.
