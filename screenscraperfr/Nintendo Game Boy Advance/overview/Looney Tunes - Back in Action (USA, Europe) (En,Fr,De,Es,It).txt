Looney Tunes Back In Action is a hilarious new adventure featuring everyone's favorite cartoon two-some, Bugs Bunny and Daffy Duck, in a globetrotting scavenger hunt to find the mythical Blue Monkey Diamond. From Hollywood to Las Vegas, from Paris to Africa, our roving heroes must run, jump, slide, and bounce their way through a heap of cartoon perils and put an end to the sinister plans of the evil ACME Corporation in an effort to save the world. "Toon Swapping" lets you switch between Bugs and Daffy at any time on your quest as you explore loads of exciting challenges on this globetrotting adventure inspired by the movie.
