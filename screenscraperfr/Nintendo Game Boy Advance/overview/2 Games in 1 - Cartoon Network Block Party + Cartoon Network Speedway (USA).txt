This double pack has two Cartoon Network GBA games combined on one cartridge: Cartoon Network Block Party: Cartoon Network Block Party is a party game that keeps the game play fast and fun on the Game Boy Advance. Play as any of 6 characters from your favorite Cartoon Network cartoon properties: Johnny Bravo, Courage the Cowardly Dog, Cow and Chicken and Ed, Edd n Eddy. Challenge up to 3 friends on 4 colorful themed boards, each styled after Cartoon Network shows: Planet Johnnywood, Katz's Creepy Castle, The Tater Farm and Extreme Cul-De-Sac.
