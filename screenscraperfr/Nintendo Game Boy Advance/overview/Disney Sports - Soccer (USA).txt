Konami has come up with DISNEY SPORTS FOOTBALL where all the Disney stars suit up to have some football fun. Play it on the Game Boy Advance and have a whale of a time. Teams face off in five-on-five player action in this arcade-style gridiron game. Each of the seven teams has its own unique statistics and play -book to keep opponents guessing. This is not for the hardcore football freak but an easy to play game for younger enthusiasts.
