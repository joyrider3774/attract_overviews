2 Games in 1: LEGO Knight's Kingdom + LEGO Bionicle was a video game compilation released in 2005 exclusively for the Nintendo Gameboy Advance. This includes the Gameboy Advance versions of LEGO Knights' Kingdom (game) and Bionicle: The Game. Both games were developed by Razorback Developments and published by THQ. There is one game cartridge with both games programmed in it to play, with a screen at the start to choose either title. Both games play the same as their original releases. The game can be played in English and German.
