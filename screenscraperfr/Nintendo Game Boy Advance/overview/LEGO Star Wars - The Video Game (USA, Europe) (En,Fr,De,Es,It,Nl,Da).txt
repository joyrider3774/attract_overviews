Lego Star Wars: The Video Game on GBA invites you to relive the trilogy of episodes I to III in the presence of the little yellow guys who can be dismantled. Rediscover the highlights of the movies and fight the Sith threat as many characters : powerful Jedi (including Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker and Master Yoda), brave droids like R2-D2 or the clumsy Binks, Chewbacca the Wookie and many others.
